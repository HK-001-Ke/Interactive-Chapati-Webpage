How to make Chapati:

Ingredients required:

  • Wheat, Eggs, Sugar, Salt, Oil, Warm Water.

Steps to follow:

  • Step no.1:Sieve the flour into a clean bowl.Ensure proper proportion to avoid inadequacy.
  • Step no.2:Mix in sugar for a sweet outcome or salt for a slightly salty chapati.Depends on target consumers.
  • Step no.3:Break an egg into the bowl and mix continually with a whisk.
  • Step no.4:Add warm water and continue mixing
  • Step no.5:Put the cutlery away and start kneading.
  • Step no.6:add oil to avoid sticking.
  • Step no.7:Once a soft,elastic dough is attained,leave it to rest.
  • Step no.8:Allow it to rest for about thirty minutes.
