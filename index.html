Chapati:

  • Chapati is a delicious Kenyan cuisine made of wheat flour and a combination of other ingredients.

  • The ingredients come together and fuse beautifully into a delicious,mouth watering wonder.

  • Oftenly reffered to as 'chapo' in Kenyan slang,the delicacy lives up to its name,but is often accompanied by beans stew,to further enhance the flavour.

  • The delicacy above can be found in vibandaskis,local restaurants but I strongly believe it should be incoorporated in major hotels as well.

  • Chapati gives out carbs yaani carbohydrates whereas beans are sources of protein

  • Chapati and Madondo(beans stew) is a filling meal which just happens to be MY FAVOURITE.
